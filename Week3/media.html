What Is WordPress

WordPress is a free and open-source content management system (CMS) that allows users to easily create and manage websites, originally designed as a blogging platform but now used for a wide variety of website types, including blogs, portfolios, e-commerce stores, and more. Here's a more detailed breakdown: Content Management System (CMS): WordPress allows users to manage and update website content without needing extensive coding knowledge. Free and Open-Source: The core WordPress software is free to use and distribute, and its open-source nature means that anyone can contribute to its development.
